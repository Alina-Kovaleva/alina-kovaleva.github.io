About Me

Hello, I am Alina, and now I dream of becoming a Front-end developer.

I’m from Russia, but I have a Dutch Residence and Work Permit.

At school I decided to connect my life with computers. Because of what, I entered the university on applied computer science and mathematics. I have a Master’s degree in computer since.

While I was studying at the university, I wanted to be a system administrator. But since I lived in a small town, where there are not many opportunities and for financial reasons, I got a job at a bank. I worked in the banking sector for about a year.
In August 2014, I found a job as a system administrator. But my husband was offered a job in Bahrain and we decided that this was a great opportunity not to be missed.

My English communication skills were nil. For this reason, I began to learn English, first on my own, later I began to attend English courses.

While we lived in Bahrain, I took various courses, also I decided to take a course on "web development" and after completing this course, I realized that it was more interesting to me than system administration. I liked creating websites and I decided to develop in this area.

Actually, the Bahrain's company promised that they would provide work for me too, but after two years in Bahrain, we realized that the company would not give me a job and we decided to look for new opportunities for ourselves.

Thus I was in the Netherlands.

My husband started working in Booking, and I continued to learn English and develop in the area of Front End development.

I have completed several intensive courses in Russian and English. And now I feel ready to work in the position of Junior Front End Developer.
